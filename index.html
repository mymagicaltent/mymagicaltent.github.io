Welcome friends, to my Magical Hairbrushing Tent!

For a long time now I’ve had a dream. To create a small space—something very intimate and beautiful, with soft textures and colours, where the light is dim and soothing, and sound from the outside world is muffled. A space where you are visually and sonically removed from your everyday concerns.

And in this space, I would invite you to rest while I brush your hair.

Hair is rich in myth, magic, history and science. Hair is rich in stories. I want the feeling of this magical tent to be like the transportative feeling of a good story, where for a time, you are taken out of yourself.

I want you to feel taken care of. That you are safe and connected to another human being.

I invite you to join me in this space from December 1 to April 1. Whether you have long hair, short hair or no hair, be at rest in my magical tent and let me pay attention to your scalp, and you.

Elisabeth xo
