Welcome friends, to my Magical Hair Brushing Tent!

For a long time now I have wanted to create a space. A small space, something very intimate and cozy. Something very beautiful with soft textures and colours. The light is dim and soothing. Sound from the outside world is muffled. You are visually and sonically removed from your everyday concerns.

When you enter, I invite you to rest in a chair and then for the next half hour I just brush your hair.

Hair is rich in legend, myth, magic, history and science. Hair is rich in stories. I want the feeling of this magical tent to be like the transportative feeling of a good story.

For a time, you are taken out of yourself.

I want you to feel taken care of. That you are safe and loved and connected to another human being.

Whether you have long hair, short hair or no hair, be at rest in my magical tent and let me pay attention to your scalp and you.
